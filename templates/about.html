About CareerPath AI

Our mission is to bridge the gap between skills and opportunities using AI-powered insights.

CareerPath AI helps students and professionals identify missing skills, map job roles, and plan their learning journey with clarity.

Whether you are exploring a new career or upskilling for your next role, the platform gives you structured, data-driven direction instead of guesswork.

What CareerPath AI Helps You Do?

  • 🔍 Discover job roles that match your current skills
  • 🧠 Identify missing skills required for your target role
  • 📊 Track your learning and career progress visually

Why CareerPath AI?

Most platforms show you job listings. CareerPath AI shows you the exact skills you need to reach those roles.

Instead of randomly learning technologies, you get focused recommendations based on real job data. This saves time, reduces confusion, and gives your learning a clear goal.

Who Is This For?

  • 🎓 Students exploring career paths
  • 💼 Professionals planning a role switch
  • 📚 Learners who want a clear skill roadmap

Built With

  • 🐍 Python for backend logic and data processing
  • 🌐 Flask for web framework and APIs
  • 🤖 Machine Learning for skill and job matching
  • 🗄️ SQL Database for storing skills, jobs, and users
  • 🎨 HTML & CSS for frontend interface
